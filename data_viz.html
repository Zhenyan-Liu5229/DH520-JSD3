Data Viz: 2019 F1 Drivers' Performance


What Is It?

This D3 data visualization is about the performances of all the Formula one drivers in 2019.

While it's easy to view the drivers' standings on the F1 official website, it's hard to get a sense of how each driver performed in each race. So I decided to look at how the standings had changed for each driver in each race and the visualization turns out to be fun.

DISCLAIMER: I am a huge fan of Max Verstappen.

How to Read/Play?

  • Use the checkboxes at the top to select the driver(s) you want to look at.
  • The X axis represents each race, and the Y axis shows the change of the standing.
  • The change of standing is calculated by [Starting Grid] - [Race Result] and all the data is from the official website.
  • Hover over each data point to see the tooltips.
  • Each driver is represented by the official color of their team.
